AMATEKA Y'INYANDIKO

Amateka ni ibanze kunyandiko Kandi inyandiko nayo nibanze kumateka, murimake abo bombi ni ababyara. kuberako inyandiko amakuru ibika niyo mateka Kandi ayo mateka niyo asubira akabika amakuru yose avuga kunyandiko, biratangaje.
inyandiko nigitangaza kuberako ibika amakuru yomubihe bitandukanye, nicyo gituma ukumenya kose biba munyandiko ndetse nokumenya inyandiko biri munyandiko, urugero rworoshye nuko jyewe ibyo nifuje kubwira uRwanda urw'umu nu urw'iyo, urw'ubu nu urw'ejo nabinyujije munyandiko none aho urihose urikubisoma ukamera nkuri kubyibwira. ibi ndikukubwira abaye Ari imitekerereze yanjye Misa birakwiriye ko nceceka kuko mvuze naba ndikuroga uRwanda, mukuru wanjye yigeze kumbwira umugani mugifaransa mubaza icyo bisigura nuko arambwira ati "udasoma aceceke" Kandi Koko yarambwiye amagambo yuzuye impanuro, yitwa Nshimyumukiza F akaba intyoza mugusoma, yampaye urugero rwiza kuko yankundishije gusoma mpora mbimushimira, rero uko nasomye kose uko, najegusanga bikwiriyeko uwasomye anandika nicyogituma nanjye nahisemo kwandika kugirango mbamenyeshe ibyerekeye imyandikire.
abantu benshi bibwirako kwandika byatangiriye muri egiputa gusa ibyo bihabanye nibiri mumateka yanditswe, abahanga benshi mubyamateka ndetse bashingiye kunyandiko basomye, ubushakashatsi bakoze burimo gucukumbura amakuru mubisigaratongo ndetse no kuganiriza abashaje, Bose bagaragazako inyandiko yambere yabayeho mumyaka Yi 3400BC ni 3300BC nicyo gihe habonetse inyandiko yabayeho mbere yizindi zose, iyo nyandiko yitwaga Cuneiform ikaba yararemwe na abacurabwenge bo mubwami bwa Mesopotamia igereranwa na Iraq yuyumunsi. iyi Cuneiform ifite amateka maremare kuberako amateka yiyinyandiko afatanye namateka yumwami wabami Sarigon wo mubwami bwa akadiya.

inyandiko yakabiri uyisanga muri Africa mugihugu cya egiputa
iyo nyandiko yabanyegiputa ifite umwihariko kuberako yifitemo ubundi buhanga buruta inyandiko ubwayo, kuberako usanga Ari inyandiko ihishe amakuru yibinyabuzima hamwe namakuru yubugingo bwaburi kinyabuzima, ntarajya kure iyi nyandiko ihambaye yitwa herogirife. herogirife ifite uburyo itandukanye nizindi nyandiko bitewe n'ubuhanga bwayo, iyinyandiko ntago bayisoma nkizindi nyandiko niyo mpamvu abahanga kubyerekeye indimi bavugako herogirife idasomwa ahubwo ifindurwa(interpreted) mbese igika cyanditswe nayo abantu bashobora kugisoma muburyo butandukanye Kandi bakanagisobanura munzira zihabanye. ikindi nuko benshi bavugako iyinyandiko ariyo nkomoko yimyandikire myinshi iriho ku isi. iyi herogirife amateka yerekanako yatangiye gukoreshwa mumyaka yi 3200BC, gusa ikintu cyingenzi kumenya nuko iyinyandiko banyirayo ubwabo bayibyajemo izindi nyandiko ebyiri zitandukanye, nuko imwe yiswe hiyerotike ariyo nyandiko yakoreshwaga nabatambyi bomuri egiputa. iyakabiri ikitwa demonike, ibi birerekanako bishobokako hariho nizindi nyandiko zaba zaravutse kubera herogirife kuko tubona nindi nyandiko yitwa koputike(coptic alphabet) , ibyo turaza kubirebera hamwe mugihe tuza kwibaza icyo arufabe aricyo ndetse naho zikomoka.

izi nyandiko ebyiri(Cuneiform na hieroglyphs) hamwe nizindi ebyiri ngiye kubabwira ntanimwe wavugako Ari iyambere kuyindi kuberako ntanimwe yiganye iyindi usibye Yuko zakozwe mubihe bitandukanye, ubwo mumvugo nziza wavugako imwe iruta indi mubyukuri bitewe nigihe yakorewe. rero iyo twise iyambere nukuvugako ariyo yakoreshejwe kera kuruta izindi. rero hariho nizindi zakozwe mubihe byakera mumico itandukanye aho tubona inyandiko ya abashinwa mubwami bwa shang-dynast mumyaka Yi 3200BC, naho mumyaka ya 900BC na 600BC nibwo dutangira kubonako mur'ibyobihe aribwo inyandiko cyangwa hieroglyphs za abamesewo-america aribo bitwa abamaya ko arico gihe batangiye gukoresha inyandiko zabo.

izi nyandiko uko Ari 4 zose zifite ibyo zihuje harimo Yuko zose zikomeye, kuziga ukazimenya bisaba ubuhanga buhambaye. ikindi usanga zari zifite uburyo ishusho imwe yabaga isobanuye interuro ya amagambo cyangwase ijambo runaka. urugero ni ijisho ryo muri hieroglyphs za abanyegiputa rishobora gusobanurwa muri ubu buryo:

ALPHABET (INYUGUTI)

nyuma Yuko isi yarimaze gukwizwa inyandiko zigoye kwiga nokumenya haje kuboneka uburyo bwiza Kandi bwizewe. ubwo buryo bwabaye ubwoko bushya bwimyandikire bubasha kwandika ijambo kurindi hakurikijwe ijwi ku ijwi, Kandi hakoreshejwe ibimenyetso bike cyane biri munsi yijana(100), aho usanga harimo utumenyetso duhagarariye inyajwi utundi ingombajwi. birumvikanako ububwoko bwimyandikire Ari bwiza kuko biroroshye kwiga ibimenyetso bike biri munsi yijana kuruta uko wakwiga ibimenyetso bibarirwa mumagana.
inyuguti(alphabet) yambere yabayeho ninayo nkomoko yizindi alphabet zose, rero inyuguti cg arufabeti zambere zakozwe na abacukuzi bamabuye yagaciro babanyakanani kandi abo banyakanani Bari abagaragu ba farawo ibyo bikagaragazako Bari bafite bike bazi kuri herogirife ya abanyagiputa. ikindi Kandi dusanga abo banyakanani inyuguti zabo ziganjemo ibimenyetso byinshi bibarirwa mumaduzeni byaba byarakuwe mubimenyetso amagana byomunyandiko ya abanyegiputa gusa abo bacukuzi bamabuye nabo baje kongeraho Andi mashushu/ibimenyetso bihimbiye ariko bayahimbye bagendeye ku umuco wabo bwite. buri inyuguti bakuraga muri hieroglyphs ntago bayandikaga nkuko yari isanzwe iteye, ahubwo bayandikaga nkaho iri mundorerwamo(barayicurikaga) barangije inyandiko yabo bayiha icyerekezo naho ubundi inyandiko ya abanyegiputa ntago yagiraga icyerekezo igenderaho iyo washakaga waheraga iburyo ujya ibumoso cyangwa ibumoso ujya iburyo rero abanyakanani ibo iyabo bayihaye icyerekezo ndahindurwa aho bandikaga bava iburyo bajya ibumoso.

birakwiriye ko twese dushimira abanyekanani kubera igihangano cyuje ubwenge bahaye isi, nibo bambere bakoze inyajwi ni ingombajwi. bukeye inyandiko yabo yaje gutwarwa na abanyefonezia(phonesia) maze bayihinduraho akantu gato gusa bayigira iyabo, bukeye bwaho ubwami bwabo bwaraguye hazamuka ubwami bwa abagereki(Greece) nuko abagereki nabo bazungura yamyandikire bayigira iyabo maze nabo bahinduraho utuntu dukeduke, bukeye bwaho habonetse Ingoma yaba eturisika(Etruscan) nabo bazungura yamyandikire yakigereki nuko bahindura utuntu dukeduke bayigira iyabo, bukeye bwaho haje ubwami bwa abaratini(Latin) nabo bazungura yamyandikire nabo bahindura cyangwa bongeraho ibintu bimwe na bimwe inyandiko iba iyabo, bukeye bwaho haje kuboneka ubwami bwubunyembaraga bwa abaromani, ubwami bwa abaroma nabwo bwaje kuzungura yamyandikire igitangaje Roma ntakintu nagito yigeze ihindura kuri yanyandiko yakiratini akaba arinayo mpamvu izi nyuguti turi gukoresha tuzitirira abaratini kuruta uko tuzitirira abaromani.
ikindi kintu nari nibagiwe kuvugaho ni ukubabwira amoko y'inyandiko ariyo Aya: nkubabitekerezo(ideograph/hieroglyphs), siraburi(syllable), abugida, arufabeti/inyuguti. siraburi usanga buri jwi rifite ishusho yaryo gusa nayo igira ibimenyetso byinshi ugereranyije na arufabe, abugida iyo igira ingombajwi gusa ntanyajwi igira. rero munyemerere mbabwireho gato kumateka ya arufabe za abaratini, izi nyuguti zakilatini nizo zambere mukwangiza no kwica burundu imico y'andimoko zikogeza imico n'indimi zabanyirazo, kuberako iyo witegereje neza mumateka usanga kubwi imbaraga zabanyirazo baragiye bigarurira amoko menshi yo mwisi. bigaruriraga ubwoko runaka binyujijwe munzira y'intambara, n'inzira y'ubukoroni, iyo basangaga mufite uburyo mwandika ururimi rwanyu bafataga burikintu kibitse iyo nyandiko  n'ubwenge bwanyu bwose bimwe bakabitwika ibindi bakabitwara nyuma yaho bakabahatira kwiga inyandiko yabo n'ururimi rwabo.
iyo basangaga muri ishyanga ridafite inyandiko barushagaho kubasuzugura no kubafata nk'inyamaswa zitagira ubwenge, maze bikarushaho kuborohera kubashyiramo imico yabo binyujijwe mumyandikire Bari kubigisha. ngaho mbwira wowe utekereza iki iyo urikumvako hariho ibihugu bimwe nabimwe byo muri Africa bishobora kuba bikoresha indimi zabyo kavukire kukigereranyo cya 32% ariko bigakoresha 68% indimi z'amahanga byumwihariko ururimi rwabakoronije. jyeweho iyo mbitekereje bintera agahinda ngatengurwa ngaterwa ubwoba nuko n'ikinyarwanda ururimi rwanjye rwiza ariho rurimo rugana. ese mumyaka 32 iri imbere nitutagira icyo dukora uribazako ikinyarwanda kizaba gikoreshwa kukigereranyo cyakangahe ku ijana?

UBUKORONI

Ubukoroni mbere na mbere menyako ari gahunda mbi Kandi cyane. ntacyiza cy'ubukoroni kuko umuntu umwe yigira nk' igitangaza uwatoranijwe n' Imana uwasizwe amavuta yubuyobozi, naho undi akamufata nk'imbwa yo kumuhigira nk'ikivume cyo kumuhingira.
ibimvuze mubisobanukiwe kundusha kuko muzi uburyo abanyafurika twacakajwe imbyaro zacu zikajyanwa bunyago Kandi muribyobihe umunyafurika yarushwaga agaciro n'imbwa y'umukoroni. ibyo singiye kubitindaho kuko kuri ubu dufite ubwigenge, gusa nabyo biri mubyatumye imico yacu yangirika Kandi s' ibyo gusa kuko nababwiyeko aho abakoroni bagiye basanga inyandiko barazitwikaga murwego rwo kurimbura umuco n'amateka y'ubwo bwoko. mu Rwanda ho murabiziko ntanyandiko twari dufite gusa ntibivuzeko twari inyamaswa ahubwo twari ishyanga rifite ubwenge buhanitse kuko abasokuruza bacu Bari bafite ubuhanga butandukanye muguhanga, ikimenyi n'ikimenyetso nuko hagati ya 2021 na 2022 ababirigi baherutse kudusubiza ibihangano byacu batwibye mugihe Bari batubase(colonial period), ibyo bihangano harimo indirimbo, imbyino, ibisigo nibindi. ibyo byerekanako twahoranye ubwege muri kamere yacu bikongera bikerekanako ubukoroni Ari isooko y'itsemba muco bukaba inyoko y'ihonya ndimi.

UBUKORONI MUKUZIMYA UMUCO NO KWIMIKA ICO

erega umuco muzima uzira ico. Kandi iyo uhawe undi muco mubyukuri uwo si umuco ahubwo ni ico. munyemerere tureberehamwe aho dusanga abakoroni barasenye umuco wabandi binyujijwe mukurimbura inyandiko:

  1. BAMUM:

    mu mwaka wi 1896 hano muri Africa mugihugu cya kameroni kungoma y'uwitwa Ibrahim Njoya Ingoma ngabe yiwe yitwaga Bamum, kumyaka yiwe 25 yamavuko mu 1896 yakusanije ibimenyetso bishoboka kugirango arememo inyandiko yandika ururimi rwiwe nabaturage biwe. mu mwaka wi 1910 nibwo iyo nyandiko yaremye iri mubwoko bwa siraburi yatangiye gukoreshwa kuko yari imaze kuzura, ifite ibimenyetso 80 bihagarariye burijwi ribarizwa m'ururimi rwabo. mugihe cy'ubukoroni igihugu cyiwe cyabanje gukoronizwa na abadage, gusa nyuma y'intambara y'isi yambere murabiziko abadage baje gutsindwa nuko ibihugu baribakoronije bigahabwa abafaransa, ababirigi nabandi.
    rero kameroni mugihe yari ikoronijwe n’abadage ntakibazo abadage bigeze bagira k'ubwenge bw'umwami Njoya ahubwo bamufashije mubikorwa bitandukanye nko gucapa inyigisho yahaga abaturage biwe harimo kwandika amateka yigihugu cyiwe, kunoza umubano ndetse namabwiriza yogukora imibonano mpuza bitsina muburyo bukwiye, nibindi. Kandi Njoya yubatse namashuri ajijura abaturage biwe, ariko nyuma y'intambara y'isi yambere.
    nico igihe kameroni yaje gukoronizwa n'abafaransa ibyakurikiyeho ni agahoma munwa, abafaransa bafashe umwami Njoya bamufungira ahantu habi kugeza bimuviriyemo urupfu, barangije bafata buri rupapuro cg ikindi cyose kiriho imyandikire ya Bamum baragitwika inyandiko yabo nibyo yari ibitse barabirimbura. maze abaturage ba kameroni bategekwa kwiga no kwandika munyuguti za kiratini (Latin alphabet) byumwihariko kwiga igifaransa. nguko uko abafaransa bagize uruhare mukwangiza umuco wabanyafurika Kandi si aho gusa, kuko natwe ibyatubayeho bafitemo uruhari singiye kubibibutsa kuko murabizi.

  2. MEITEI MAYEK:

    iyi nyandiko meyiteyi mayeke bivugwako ishabora kuba yarakoreshejwe igihe kingana n'ikinyejana cg ikinyagihumbi. yari inyandiko yakoreshwaga yandika ururimi rwita Manipuri mumajyaruguru y'iburasirazuba bw'Ubuhinde. mumwaka wi 1709 abamanipuri baje kwinjirirwa n'umuvugabutumwa wo mubwoko bwa abahindu maze abuzuzamo inyigisho zagihindu bita VAISHNAVISM Kandi iyo myizerere yabaga ifite ibitabo byanditswe munyuguti z'abahindu zitwa BENGALI. mu 1729 umwami w'abamanipuri warumaze guhinduka mumyizerere kubera kumva inyigisho za gihindu yaje kugoryama nuko ategekako batwika buri kintu cyose Kiri munyandiko ya MEITEI MAYEK, Kandi Koko yaje kubigeraho inyandiko y'ubwoko n'ururimi rwiwe arabirimbura. igikorwa yakoze niba uri umunyarwanda usobanukiweko ibyo yakoze ko biruta gutema ishami wicayeho, ahubwo yatemye umugongo umuhekera, yaciye amabere y'Inka imukamirwa! iyinkuru icyo dukwiriye kuyikuramo nuko Idini naryo rishobora kugira uruhare muguhonya umuco runaka nibyawo byose Kandi iyo binyuze mumyizerere ikintu gitangaje nuko ntagahato ushyirwaho ngo wisenyere, ahubwo ubikora ubishaka Kandi ubikunze maze ukiyangiza kakahava.

UBWIGENGE

UBWIGENGE niyo ntwaro irwanya ububata(ubukoroni) Kandi nicyo gisubizo cyo kunesha ububata.
hano ndagirango tureberehamwe ibihugu bitandukanye ku isi(ntago Ari muri Africa gusa) byagiye bitahurako kuba bikoresha inyandiko z'abandi byumwihariko zakilatini ariyo nzira yo kurimbuka ku ururimi rwabo n' umuco wabo. bagahitamo gusigasira umuco wabo binyuze muguha ururimi rwabo inyandiko yarwo yihariye Kandi bwite bwitiriwe ubwoko bwabo. ikindi ntutekerezeko izo mpinduramatwara zabaye mubihe twita antikite. Hoya rwose, turaza gusanga biboneka ndetse nomumyaka yo mubihumbi bibiri byaragiye bikorwa gusa turahera mumyaka ya kera tuza twegera ibihe turimo.

    Armenia nyuma yo gukoronizwa (kubatwa) n'amoko atandukanye(Persians, Arabs, Byzantines, Mongols and Turks) gusa buri bwoko bwababase bwabahaga inyandiko yabwo ngo bayikoreshe ndetse no kururimi rwabo. hariho umugabo wariwarabaye umukirisito kurwego rukomeye aho nanubu igihugu cyiwe kimwita umutagatifu Kandi uwo mugabo yakoze imirimo myinshi ngo igihugu cyiwe kibone ubwigenge mubintu bitandukanye kuko bivugwako yatanze umusanzu ukomeye mukubaka ubwami bw'Arumeniya bwitwaga Arsacid dynasty cg arishakuni. ntawundi uwo yitwa Mesrop mashtots , Mesrop yaje kugira intekerezo imwerekako ururimi rwabo ruzarimbuka nirubaho rudafite inyandiko yarwo ihamye Kandi yihariye kurirwo, Kandi ko bikwiriyeko igihugu cyiwe kigaragara neza nkibindi bihugu byose bikomeye ikindi abonako rubanda rugufi rutagize amahirwe yo kwiga bihambaye nabo bakwiye gusoma nokwandika ururimi rwabo, byumwihariko nkuwihaye Imana wese yari afite intego yo gucapa bibiriya n'ururimi rwabo rw'Arumeniya munyandiko yarwo bwite. igitekerezo cyiwe umwami warikungoma yarigishimye nuko mumwaka wa 405 nibwo yasoje uyu mukoro utoroshye, nuko inyandiko yabo itangira gukoreshwa.
    magingo aya inyandiko yabo yabaye ubukombe kandi Arumeniya ibarwa mubihugu bikomeye ndetse bifite inyandiko ibiranga ku isi. nkuko Mesrop yabirotaga.

    tugere muri aziya dutere ijisho muri Korea kungoma yumwami Sejong the Great

  3. N'KO ALPHABET:

    kera habayeho umugabo akitwa Solomana Kante wari utuye mugace kitwa Kankan mugihugu cya Guinea. uyu Kante yaje gusoma ikinyamakuru gisebya abanyafurika muri rusange, iyo nkuru idusebya ikatwaka ubumuntu yari yaranditswe munkuru z'ikinyamakuru cyitwa Lebanese igira iti indimi nyafurika zimeze nk'indimi zinyoni(inyamaswa) zose ntibishobokako zandikwa. abihuza nukuntu abirabura bafatwaga Koko nkinyamaswa bimutera umwete wokunyomoza inkurukinyoma nkiyongiyo, nuko mugihe yari yarasuhukiye mugihugu cya kotedivwari nibwo yatangiye kurema inyandiko yokwandika ururimi rwiwe rwitwa Mandenkana (rubarizwa muri Mande languages) rukoreshwa mubihugu bitandukanye byomuri afrika yiburengera zuba.
    mu 1949 nibwo yasoje uyu mukoro, nuko ataha iwabo yamamaza umurimo wintoki ziwe abaturage barabishima kuko babonyeko amata abyaye amavuta.
    kandi Bari bafite umugani mumuco wabo uvuga nkagutya uti "igisenge gisakaye inzu imwe nticyasakara indi (the roof of a hut from one village, will never fit a hut in a different village)" bikabafasha kumvako inyandiko yandika ururimi rumwe bidakwiyeko yandika nurundi bitandukanye. iyi nyandiko yayise nko bisobanurwa gutya ngo "ndavuze" magingo Aya nko n'inyandiko ikoreshwa mubihugu bitandukanye nka Mali, Guinea, Gambia, Côte d’Ivoire, Burkina Faso, Senegal, Liberia, Guinea-Bissau and Sierra Leone byumwihariko kubakoresha ururimi rwa mandinkana.
    nko ikoreshwa byumwihariko muri gineya ikindi iyi nyandiko yaje gukoreshwa cyangwa kwemerwa ko yajya muzindi nyandiko zikoreshwa muri mudasobwa(computers) mumwaka wi 2006 nyakanga kubera ubufasha bwa UNICODE consortium y'abanyamerica.
